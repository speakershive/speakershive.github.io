About us

Speakers Hive is a non-profit community that helps its members to advance their career, by equipping them with communication and leadership skills. The community adopts a "learn-by-doing" methodology for developing skills.

We follow two learning paths :

Speech Mastery Program is for members to develop their communication skills.

Leadership Mastery Program is for members to develop their leadership skills.

Speakers Hive can be established in colleges, communities and colleges.

Vision

To create a positive change in communities, by developing individuals who can communicate effectively and lead people with empathy.

Mission

To provide a fun-filled learning environment for communication and leadership skills development.
